"Which AI tool should we buy?"

through before they invest — so we turned them into a free, self-scored guide:

And I'm genuinely curious — if you run a growing company: what's holding your
AI plans back more right now, the technology or the operations underneath it?

#AIReadiness #SMB #Operations #Leadership
