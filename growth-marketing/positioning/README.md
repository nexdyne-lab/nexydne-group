# Brand Positioning

The daily-lead message for NexDyne, and the competitive research behind it.

- **`nexdyne-positioning`** (.md/.docx/.pdf) — THE daily lead: "We enhance human
  intelligence to drive innovation in the age of AI." Where HIG fits (the how,
  not the why), the three foils (vs institutions / AI vendors / traditional
  consultancies), message pillars, say-this/not-that, ready-to-use lines.
- **`stanford-hai-research`** (.md/.docx/.pdf) — competitive analysis of Stanford
  HAI: their exact mission/vision language, their question-led institutional
  voice, and the strategic gap a corporation should exploit (assert & advance
  vs study & reassure).
- **`build_docs.py`** — regenerates all three formats from one structured source
  (run it, then render the .html to .pdf via Puppeteer). Edit content here so
  md/docx/pdf never drift.

Core distinction: HIG (Human Intelligence, Governed) is our governance framework
— the HOW. The daily lead is the value proposition — the WHY: enhancing human
intelligence to drive innovation. Lead with the why; support with HIG.
