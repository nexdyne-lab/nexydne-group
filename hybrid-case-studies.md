## Strategy
Show capability to serve companies at various growth stages:
1. **Mid-market** (100-300 employees) - Established operations, complex processes
2. **Growth-stage SME** (50-150 employees) - Scaling rapidly, automation critical
3. **Small SME** (25-50 employees) - Entry point, accessible, relatable

---

## Case Study 1: Mid-Market Logistics Company
**Industry:** Logistics & Supply Chain  
**Company Type:** Regional logistics provider  
**Employees:** 180-220

### Title
**92% reduction in shipment processing errors**

### Summary
Regional logistics provider with 15 distribution centers automates freight coordination and tracking, processing 5,000+ shipments monthly with near-zero errors.

### The Challenge
A regional logistics company managing freight across 15 distribution centers struggled with manual shipment coordination, invoice processing, and real-time tracking updates. Staff manually entered data from multiple carrier systems, leading to frequent errors, delayed shipments, and customer complaints. With 5,000+ shipments monthly, the manual process consumed 120+ staff hours weekly and created bottlenecks that prevented scaling to new markets.

### The Solution
NEXDYNE implemented an intelligent logistics automation platform:
- **Multi-carrier integration** automatically pulling shipment data from 12+ carrier APIs
- **Automated invoice reconciliation** matching shipments to billing with AI-powered validation
- **Real-time tracking orchestration** updating customers across all channels simultaneously
- **Predictive delay detection** forecasting delivery issues 24-48 hours in advance

### The Results
- **92%** reduction in processing errors
- **5,000+** shipments processed monthly with automated workflows
- **120** staff hours saved per week
- **$340K** annual savings from error reduction and efficiency gains

## Case Study 2: Growth-Stage SaaS Company
**Industry:** Software & Technology  
**Company Type:** B2B SaaS platform  
**Employees:** 85-110

### Title
**78% faster customer onboarding for scaling SaaS platform**

### Summary
Fast-growing B2B SaaS company automates customer onboarding and provisioning, reducing setup time from 5 days to 26 hours while scaling to 400+ new customers quarterly.

### The Challenge
A rapidly scaling SaaS platform faced onboarding bottlenecks as customer acquisition accelerated from 50 to 400+ new clients per quarter. Manual account provisioning, custom configuration, data migration, and training coordination required 5-7 days per customer. The 12-person customer success team couldn't keep pace, creating a backlog that threatened growth targets and customer satisfaction.

### The Solution
NEXDYNE deployed an intelligent onboarding automation system:
- **Automated account provisioning** creating configured environments in under 2 hours
- **Self-service configuration wizards** guiding customers through setup with AI assistance
- **Intelligent data migration** automatically mapping and importing customer data
- **Automated training scheduling** coordinating personalized onboarding sessions

### The Results
- **78%** faster onboarding (5 days → 26 hours)
- **400+** customers onboarded quarterly without adding headcount
- **95%** customer satisfaction score for onboarding experience
- **$180K** annual savings from efficiency gains

## Case Study 3: Small Dental Practice Group
**Industry:** Healthcare Services  
**Company Type:** Multi-location dental practice  
**Employees:** 28-35

### Title
**70% reduction in appointment no-shows through automated patient engagement**

### Summary
Family dental practice with 4 locations deploys intelligent patient communication automation, saving 15 hours per week per location and recovering $45K annually.

### The Challenge
A dental practice group with 4 locations faced high no-show rates (18-22%), manual appointment scheduling consuming staff time, and inconsistent patient follow-up. Receptionists spent 40% of their time on phone calls for appointment reminders, rescheduling, and insurance verification. The practice needed to improve patient engagement while reducing administrative overhead to focus on patient care.

### The Solution
NEXDYNE deployed an intelligent patient engagement platform:
- **Automated appointment reminders** via SMS, email, and voice calls
- **AI-powered scheduling assistant** handling rescheduling requests 24/7
- **Insurance verification automation** checking coverage before appointments
- **Intelligent follow-up sequences** for post-treatment care and recall appointments

### The Results
- **70%** reduction in no-shows (20% → 6%)
- **15** hours saved per week per location
- **$45K** annual revenue recovered from reduced no-shows
- **94%** patient satisfaction with automated communications
