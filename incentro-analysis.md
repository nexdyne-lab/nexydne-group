## Key Design Patterns

1. **Two-column layouts** - Consistent pattern of image/content or content/content splits
2. **Diagonal dividers** - Used between sections for visual interest
3. **Icon-based benefit cards** - Simple icons with headings and descriptions
4. **Clickable service cards** - With arrow indicators showing interactivity
5. **Color scheme** - Burgundy, orange, white, light blue
6. **Typography** - Bold headings, clean sans-serif body text
7. **CTA placement** - Multiple "Get in touch" buttons throughout
8. **Visual metaphors** - Chess pieces for strategy, warm lighting for collaboration

## Content Strategy

1. **Problem → Solution flow** - Starts with benefits, then explains methodology
2. **Framework introduction** - BOAT concept as differentiator
3. **Service integration** - Links to specific services (Process Automation, App Dev, Data)
4. **Social proof** - Case studies section
5. **Multiple conversion points** - CTAs throughout the page

## Key Takeaways for NEXDYNE Page

- Use two-column layouts for content variety
- Include benefits section with icons (4 key benefits)
- Explain methodology/framework (for NEXDYNE: AI-driven approach)
- Link to related services
- Include case studies
- Multiple CTAs throughout
- Use visual metaphors and professional imagery
- Maintain consistent spacing and typography
