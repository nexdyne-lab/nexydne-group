Insights Section Design Reference (from image):

Layout:
- Clean white/cream background
- "LATEST INSIGHTS" label at top center (uppercase, letter-spaced)
- Large serif heading "Thinking that drives transformation." centered
- Three column grid for article cards

Card Design:
- Each card has a thin top border (separator line)
- Category label on left (STRATEGY, TECHNOLOGY, OPERATIONS) - uppercase, small, muted
- Read time on right (5 min read, 7 min read, 10 min read) - small, muted
- Article title below - larger, bold, serif font
- Middle card (TECHNOLOGY) has teal/cyan colored title link
- "Read article →" link at bottom of each card

Colors:
- Background: off-white/cream (#F7F5F0 or similar)
- Text: dark gray/black
- Category labels: muted gray
- Middle article title: teal/cyan (#0077B5 or similar)
- Other titles: black

Typography:
- "LATEST INSIGHTS" - uppercase, letter-spaced, small
- Main heading - large serif, bold
- Category - uppercase, small, tracking
- Read time - small, regular weight
- Article title - medium-large, serif, bold
- "Read article" - small, with arrow
