**Industry:** Government / Public Sector  
**Challenge:** Manual compliance documentation for cloud security authorizations taking 6-12 months  
**Solution:** Automated compliance monitoring and documentation generation system  
**Results:**
- **78% reduction in authorization timeline** (from 6 months to 6 weeks)
- Real-time security posture monitoring replacing quarterly manual reviews
- Automated generation of 80% of required compliance documentation

**Description:** A federal agency partnered with NEXDYNE to automate their FedRAMP compliance process.
