## Case Study: Growth-Stage E-commerce Company
**Industry:** E-commerce & Retail  
**Company Type:** Multi-channel online retailer  
**Employees:** 95-120

### Title
**83% reduction in order fulfillment errors**

### Summary
Fast-growing e-commerce retailer with 8 warehouses automates order processing and inventory management, handling 12,000+ daily orders across 4 sales channels with near-perfect accuracy.

### The Challenge
A rapidly expanding e-commerce company selling across Amazon, Shopify, eBay, and their own website struggled with order synchronization, inventory tracking, and fulfillment coordination across 8 warehouse locations. Manual order entry from multiple platforms led to overselling, shipping delays, and frequent customer complaints. With order volume growing from 3,000 to 12,000+ daily orders in 18 months, the 15-person operations team couldn't keep pace. Fulfillment errors reached 17%, threatening the company's marketplace ratings and growth trajectory.

### The Solution
NEXDYNE implemented an intelligent order management and fulfillment automation platform:
- **Multi-channel order orchestration** automatically syncing orders from 4 sales platforms in real-time
- **Intelligent inventory allocation** optimizing stock distribution across 8 warehouses based on demand patterns
- **Automated fulfillment routing** directing orders to optimal warehouse locations for fastest shipping
- **Real-time inventory synchronization** preventing overselling across all channels
- **Automated shipping label generation** and carrier selection based on cost and delivery speed

### The Results
- **83%** reduction in fulfillment errors (17% → 3%)
- **12,000+** daily orders processed automatically across 4 channels
- **8** warehouse locations coordinated in real-time
- **$280K** annual savings from error reduction and efficiency gains
- **4.8/5** average marketplace rating (up from 3.9/5)
