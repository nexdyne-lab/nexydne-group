# Consulting Firm Service Page Research

## McKinsey Growth, Marketing & Sales Page Structure

### Key Sections Identified:
1. **Hero/Introduction** - Brief value proposition about growth transformations
2. **Featured Case Studies** - Real client success stories with specific outcomes
3. **Our People** - Senior partners with expertise and credentials
4. **Featured Capabilities** - Specific tools and platforms (Periscope, CustomerOne, Experience DNA, Marketing Solutions)
5. **Impact Stories** - Short case study snippets with metrics
6. **Featured Publication** - Thought leadership content
7. **Career CTA** - Recruitment section

### Content Patterns:
- Case studies mention specific companies (Türkiye, Deutsche Telekom, Ascendum)
- Metrics are specific (20% jump in sales, 30% increase in profits)
- Tools/platforms have clear value propositions
- Senior partners listed with locations and expertise areas
- Publications drive thought leadership credibility

### Design Elements:
- Clean, professional layout
- Case study cards with images
- People section with photos and bios
- Capability cards with descriptions
- Impact stories as brief snippets


## Bain Sales & Marketing Page Structure

### Key Sections Identified:
1. **Hero** - "Put customers first to power your growth"
2. **Key Metrics** - 2-4x increase in top-line growth, 10 p.p. uplift in productivity, 25% top-line growth
3. **Value Proposition** - Sustainable organic growth, customer-centric strategy
4. **Methodology** - Net Promoter System, customer journeys, breakthrough research
5. **AI Integration** - How AI transforms sales (30% improvement in win rates)
6. **Use Cases** - 25 high-potential use cases across sales life cycle
7. **Industry Experience** - Consumer products, retail, banking, energy, education, sports
8. **Featured Publication** - Book promotion
9. **Insights/Newsletter** - Subscribe to industry-specific content

### Content Patterns:
- Specific metrics with percentages and multipliers
- Clear methodology (Net Promoter System)
- AI integration prominently featured
- Industry breadth mentioned
- Thought leadership publications
- Newsletter subscription CTA

## Key Takeaways for NexDyne Pages:

### Essential Sections for Capability Pages:
1. **Hero** - Clean, professional with clear value proposition
2. **Key Metrics** - 3-4 impressive statistics with context
3. **Narrative** - Why this matters, market context
4. **Core Capabilities** - 4-6 service areas with descriptions
5. **Case Studies** - 2-3 real examples with metrics
6. **Methodology/Approach** - Proprietary frameworks or tools
7. **Featured Insight** - Thought leadership content
8. **CTA** - Clear next step for conversion

### Content Standards:
- Metrics should be specific and credible
- Case studies need company context and outcomes
- Methodology adds credibility
- AI integration is expected in modern consulting
- Industry experience builds trust
