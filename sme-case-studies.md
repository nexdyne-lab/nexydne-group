# SME-Focused Case Studies for NEXDYNE

## Case Study 1: E-commerce / Retail SME
**Industry:** E-commerce & Retail  
**Company Type:** Mid-sized online retailer  
**Employees:** 45-60

### Title
**85% faster order processing with automated workflows**

### Summary
Mid-sized e-commerce retailer eliminates manual order processing bottlenecks, reducing fulfillment time from 48 hours to 7 hours with intelligent automation.

### The Challenge
A growing e-commerce retailer processing 500-800 orders daily struggled with manual order entry, inventory updates, and shipping coordination across multiple sales channels (website, marketplace platforms). Staff spent 6-8 hours daily on repetitive data entry, leading to delayed shipments, inventory errors, and customer complaints. The company needed to scale operations without proportionally increasing headcount.

### The Solution
NEXDYNE implemented an intelligent automation platform that transformed order management:
- **Multi-channel order aggregation** automatically consolidating orders from all sales platforms
- **Automated inventory synchronization** updating stock levels in real-time across all channels
- **Intelligent shipping automation** selecting optimal carriers and generating labels automatically
- **AI-powered fraud detection** flagging suspicious orders for manual review

### The Results
- **85%** faster order processing (48h → 7h)
- **500+** orders processed daily without errors
- **3.5** FTE hours saved per day
- **92%** reduction in inventory discrepancies

## Case Study 2: Healthcare SME
**Industry:** Healthcare Services  
**Company Type:** Multi-location dental practice  
**Employees:** 25-35

### Title
**70% reduction in appointment no-shows through automated patient engagement**

### Summary
Regional dental practice with 4 locations deploys intelligent automation for patient communication, reducing no-shows and administrative workload.

### The Challenge
A dental practice group with 4 locations faced high no-show rates (18-22%), manual appointment scheduling consuming staff time, and inconsistent patient follow-up. Receptionists spent 40% of their time on phone calls for appointment reminders, rescheduling, and insurance verification. The practice needed to improve patient engagement while reducing administrative overhead.

### The Solution
NEXDYNE deployed an intelligent patient engagement platform:
- **Automated appointment reminders** via SMS, email, and voice calls
- **AI-powered scheduling assistant** handling rescheduling requests 24/7
- **Insurance verification automation** checking coverage before appointments
- **Intelligent follow-up sequences** for post-treatment care and recall appointments

### The Results
- **70%** reduction in no-shows (20% → 6%)
- **15** hours saved per week per location
- **$45K** annual revenue recovered from reduced no-shows
- **94%** patient satisfaction with automated communications

## Case Study 3: Manufacturing SME
**Industry:** Light Manufacturing  
**Company Type:** Custom parts manufacturer  
**Employees:** 35-50

### Title
**$180K annual savings from predictive maintenance and quality automation**

### Summary
Small custom parts manufacturer implements IoT-powered predictive maintenance and automated quality control, reducing downtime and defects.

### The Challenge
A custom parts manufacturer producing components for automotive and aerospace clients faced frequent unexpected equipment breakdowns causing production delays and missed deadlines. Manual quality inspections caught defects late in the process, resulting in material waste and rework costs. The company needed to improve reliability and quality without hiring additional staff.

### The Solution
NEXDYNE implemented an intelligent operations platform:
- **IoT sensor integration** on critical CNC machines for real-time monitoring
- **Predictive maintenance alerts** forecasting failures 3-5 days in advance
- **Computer vision quality control** inspecting 100% of parts in real-time
- **Automated production scheduling** optimizing machine utilization

### The Results
- **$180K** annual savings (maintenance + waste reduction)
- **65%** reduction in unplanned downtime
- **98.5%** first-pass quality rate (up from 87%)
- **30%** increase in on-time deliveries

---

## Key Themes for SME Case Studies
- Realistic scale: 25-60 employees, not thousands
- Tangible ROI: Specific dollar amounts ($45K, $180K) not millions
- Practical challenges: Manual processes, scaling issues, resource constraints
- Measurable outcomes: Time saved, error reduction, revenue impact
- Relatable industries: Retail, healthcare services, light manufacturing
